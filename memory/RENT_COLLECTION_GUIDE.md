# DOMMMA Rent Collection Platform

## Overview

DOMMMA provides a comprehensive rent collection platform that streamlines the payment process between landlords and tenants. This document answers common questions about how the system works.

---

## How Rent Collection Works

### For Landlords

1. **Set Up Payment Collection**
   - Navigate to Dashboard → Payments → Payment Methods
   - Add your bank account or credit card to receive payments
   - Set up automatic rent reminders for your tenants

2. **Create Rent Invoices**
   - Go to Pay & Invoices section
   - Click "Create Invoice" and select the tenant
   - Enter rent amount, due date, and any additional fees
   - Invoice is automatically sent to tenant via email

3. **Track Payments**
   - View real-time payment status in your dashboard
   - See which tenants have paid, pending, or overdue
   - Download payment history reports

### For Renters

1. **Pay Rent**
   - Navigate to Pay & Invoices in your dashboard
   - View all pending and past invoices
   - Click "Pay Now" to make a payment
   - Choose credit card, debit card, or bank transfer

2. **Set Up Auto-Pay**
   - Enable automatic payments in Settings → Payment Methods
   - Rent is automatically charged on the due date
   - Receive confirmation emails for each payment

## Payment Reminders

### Automatic Reminders

DOMMMA sends automatic payment reminders to tenants:

| Reminder Type | Timing | Method |
|---------------|--------|--------|
| Upcoming Rent Due | 5 days before due date | Email + Push Notification |
| Rent Due Today | On due date | Email + Push Notification |
| Payment Overdue | 1 day after due date | Email + SMS (if enabled) |
| Second Reminder | 3 days after due date | Email + SMS |
| Final Notice | 7 days after due date | Email + SMS + In-App Alert |

### Custom Reminders

Landlords can customize reminder settings:
- Set custom reminder intervals
- Enable/disable SMS notifications
- Add personal messages to reminders
- Configure grace periods (1-7 days)

## Late Payment Policies

### How Late Payments Are Handled

1. **Grace Period (Default: 3 days)**
   - No late fees during grace period
   - Reminder emails sent daily
   - Payment status shows as "Due"

2. **After Grace Period**
   - Late fee can be applied (landlord configurable)
   - Status changes to "Overdue"
   - Urgent notification sent to tenant

3. **Severely Overdue (14+ days)**
   - Multiple notifications sent
   - Account flagged for landlord review
   - Option to initiate collection process

### Late Fee Configuration

Landlords can set late fees in their property settings:
- **Flat Fee:** Fixed amount (e.g., $50)
- **Percentage:** Percentage of rent (e.g., 5%)
- **Daily Rate:** Amount per day late (e.g., $10/day)
- **Maximum Cap:** Maximum late fee amount

## Impact on User Ratings

### How Payments Affect Ratings

DOMMMA's rating system considers payment history:

**For Tenants:**
- On-time payments: Positive impact on rating
- Occasional late payments (1-2): Minor impact
- Frequent late payments: Significant negative impact
- Defaults/Non-payment: Severe impact

**For Landlords:**
- Proper fee disclosure: Required for good standing
- Timely issue resolution: Positive impact
- Unfair fee practices: Negative reviews

### Rating Transparency

- Payment history is visible on profiles (summary only)
- Specific amounts are never disclosed publicly
- Tenants can explain extenuating circumstances
- Dispute resolution available for unfair ratings

## Payment Recipient Verification

### How We Ensure Payments Go to the Right Person

1. **Identity Verification**
   - Landlords must verify email and phone
   - ID verification required for payouts over $1,000
   - Bank account ownership verified via micro-deposits

2. **Property Ownership Verification**
   - Landlords can verify property ownership
   - Claim listing feature with document upload
   - Staff review for disputed properties

3. **Payout Security**
   - All payouts go to verified bank accounts only
   - 48-hour hold on first payout to new accounts
   - Fraud monitoring on all transactions

### Dispute Resolution

If a payment is sent to the wrong recipient:
- Report within 72 hours via Dashboard → Support
- Investigation completed within 5 business days
- Full refund if fraud is confirmed
- Mediation available for disputes

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I pay rent with a credit card?**
A: Yes! Credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers are all accepted. Credit cards incur a 2.9% + $0.30 processing fee.

**Q: What happens if my payment fails?**
A: You'll receive an immediate notification. Retry your payment within 24 hours to avoid late fees. Contact support if issues persist.

**Q: Can landlords see my full payment history?**
A: Landlords can only see payments made to them. Your payment history with other landlords is private.

**Q: How do I dispute a charge?**
A: Go to Pay & Invoices → Select the charge → Click "Dispute" → Fill out the form. We'll investigate within 5 business days.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No hidden fees. All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm a payment.
